

**As I begin to love myself,**  
当我开始爱自己，  

I learn to listen—to the quiet voice within  
that whispers truth louder than the world’s noise.  
我学会了倾听——内心那个安静的声音，  
它比世界的喧嚣更响亮地诉说着真相。  

I forgive the mistakes of yesterday,  
not because I was weak,  
but because I was learning.  
我原谅了昨天的错误，  
不是因为我软弱，  
而是因为我在成长、在学习。  

I stop chasing perfection,  
and start embracing my flaws—  
the cracks where the light gets in.  
我不再追逐完美，  
而是开始接纳自己的缺陷——  
那些让光照进来的裂缝。  

I honor my needs,  
not as selfish demands,  
but as sacred signs of self-respect.  
我尊重自己的需要，  
不是将它们视为自私的要求，  
而是当作自尊的神圣信号。  

I allow myself to rest,  
to feel, to grow,  
without apology or guilt.  
我允许自己休息、感受、成长，  
无需道歉或感到愧疚。  

And slowly,  
like the sun rising after a long night,  
I begin to shine—not for anyone else,  
but simply because I am.  
慢慢地，  
就像长夜之后升起的太阳，  
我开始闪耀——不是为了任何人，  
而只是因为，我就是我。
